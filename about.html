Antonio Tully-Spinozzi
is a Graphic Designer living in NY.

Design professional with over fifteen years of experience running a design start-up that matured into the creative development of music industry clients.

Supervised a creative team to develop the brands for emerging artists to take to market. Successfully produced design projects for clients featured in national campaigns on the BBC, Uncut Magazine, Microsoft Bing, etc.

Key strengths include: building and maintaining trusted relationships with project managers to understand their needs, developing creative strategies to accomplish goals, managing cross-functional teams, and adhering to standards of accountability and continuous improvement. Independently sets priorities to manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment. Excellent leadership, organization, and communication skills, with experience in the creative, product design, and tech sectors.
