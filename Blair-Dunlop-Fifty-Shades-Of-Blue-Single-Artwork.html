Initially, I worked with the folk singer/songwriter Blair Dunlop on the artwork for the release of his single Fifty Shades of Blue.

At this point in his music career, his brand lacked definition. I worked towards the development of one that would target a broader audience.

For the logo, I took inspiration from design forms found in architecture and interior design from the Italian Renaissance period.

Finally, I tackled his website.


PROJECT: Graphic Design, Logo Design, Website Design and Development
CLIENT: Blair Dunlop
