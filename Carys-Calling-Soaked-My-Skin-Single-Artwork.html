The singer/songwriter Carys Calling came to me with a photo she wished to be on the cover of her debut single, Soaked My Skin.

As sometimes can happen in the design process, I didn't particularly agree that the photo was the most appropriate for the end product.

I consulted with Carys about the direction I wished to take the piece. I wanted to focus on a particular element of the image, a reflection of Carys in a store window and make it more ethereal. I felt this was better suited for her song writing style.


PROJECT: Graphic Design, Photo Editing
CLIENT: Carys Calling
